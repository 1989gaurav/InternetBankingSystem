Following are the frequently asked questions about IBS.

·     Q- Can i map an account to my user profile.
A- Yes, You can map an account to your user profile if you are one of the account holder for that bank account and that account is in our bank.

·     Q- What is the use of Virtual Keyboard ?.
A- The virtual keyboard prevent your account from KeyLogger attack. A KeyLogger can detect the keyboard keys pressed by you and using these keys, an attacker can hack your account.

·     Q- What is this Enable/Disable security option ?.
A- This option allows you to Enable high security on your account. On enabling this feature, you will be asked for confirmation by SMS before transaction.

·     Q- Why cant i update my Name and Address ?.
A- Changing the Name and Address will leave the system in inconsistent state. For this reason, we can not allow a user to change his/her Name or Address.

·     Q- What is account summary and account statement ?.
A- Account summary shows all accounts mapped to your profile including loan accounts. While, Account statement shows all transactions done by you on a perticular account within a time period.

·     Q- What is the difference between Intra-Banking and Inter-Banking ?.
A- Intra-Banking is a banking facility or transaction facility between two accounts of a Same bank (our bank) while Inter-Banking allows you make transactions between accounts of different banks.

·     Q- What is an Standing Instruction ?.
A- Standing instruction is an instruction given by you to the bank to transfer some amount of money from one of your mapped account to some other accounts on a regular interval. This service also allows you to pay your loan EMI on regular intervals.
