Internet Banking System is a service being offered by many banks today. With the provision of such a service, it has become very easy for a customer to avail the services offered by a bank. Today, it is very easy to view one's account balance, get passbook entries or Account Statements, transferring money all over the globe.

Today, Banks have also partnered with many third party service providers. One can pay his telephone bills, electricity bills and many others being at home. One can use his Internet Banking Account to shop over the web. One can access his DEMAT Accounts through this and can do much more.

From business point of view, an entrepreneur or an industrialist might need large amounts of money to be transferred almost daily. They also need Letters of Credit & Guarantee to make Import & Export Business.
